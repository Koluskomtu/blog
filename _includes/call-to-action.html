Who am I?

I am an artist with many interests none of which I'd say I've developed into a professional level. This blog is an attempt to shed a spotlight on those interests in the form of articles pertaining to past and future projects. The incentive for building a personal blog is also to get the word out there for others to collaborate with and participate. If you yourself are an artist and communicate or may have experiences that you may want to add to the table, by all means, contact me. I will clarify in some of my articles in the portfolio section of my 3d records project the need for C++ programmers and game engine environmental artists, as well as, share project code on GitHub with would be artists. Enjoy the blog and take care.
